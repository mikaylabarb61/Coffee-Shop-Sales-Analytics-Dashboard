# Coffee Shop Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes 2023 coffee shop sales data using Tableau and Excel. The objective was to identify revenue trends, product performance, customer purchasing behavior, and sales patterns across locations.

## Tools Used

* Tableau
* Microsoft Excel
* Data Visualization
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Business Questions

* Which product categories generate the highest sales volume?
* Which products contribute the most revenue?
* How does purchasing behavior vary throughout the day?
* Which store locations generate the highest sales revenue?
* What products account for the largest share of sales?

## Dashboard Components

### Sales Revenue Analysis

* Revenue by store location
* Revenue by product category
* Product sales contribution analysis

### Product Performance Analysis

* Quantity sold by product category
* Quantity sold by individual product
* Share of sales by product

### Customer Purchasing Trends

* Coffee and tea purchases by time of day
* Demand patterns across product categories
